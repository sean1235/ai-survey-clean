Survey on Using AI Tools (e.g., DeepSeek, ChatGPT) to Solve Daily Problems

同学你好！本问卷想了解你用AI工具解决生活中实际问题的情况。大约需3分钟。匿名，只用于研究。
Hello! This survey asks how you use AI tools to solve real-life problems. It takes about 3 minutes. Anonymous, only for research. Thank you!

请填写您所在的地区 / Please enter your region
(例如：北京、上海、广州、成都等 / e.g., Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chengdu, etc.)

13. 你对用AI学中文还有什么建议吗？（选填） / Do you have any other suggestions for learning Chinese with AI? (Optional)
